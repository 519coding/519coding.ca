Making a website with a website builder vs coding it yourself

While there are many website builders such as Weebly and Wix that make it super easy to make your own website, these website builders limit your freedom to customize your website's design and features. Having said that, learning how to code a website from scratch, without the help of website builders gives you more freedom to design your website the way you want it and give it customized features.
